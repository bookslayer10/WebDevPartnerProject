Use W, A, S, D to navigate around the map
Use E to zoom into the map and Q to zoom out.

To move or fire a unit, first click to select it, then click on the hex you want to move to/shoot.
Then, you can toggle between movement and shooting with the toggle button at the top of the screen.
Every one of your units gets a set ammount of actions per turn, which can be spent on either movement or firing.

Infantry - Range:2 - Health:3 - Actions:2
Armour - Range:2 - Health:4 - Actions:3
Artillery - Range:5 - Health:2 - Actions:1




Destroy the enemy base using your pieces, infantry, tanks, and artillery while protecting your own base. You can destroy enemy pieces to clear your way to the base.
